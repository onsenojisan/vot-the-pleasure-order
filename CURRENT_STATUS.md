# Valence Order Theory / The Pleasure Order: Current Status

Date: 2026-06-19  
Status: public-facing boundary summary  

For navigation across public documents, see [Reader Guide](READER_GUIDE.md).

## One-Sentence Status

Valence Order Theory is currently a coherent and testable research program, not a validated theory.

## Minimal Current Claim

The current bounded claim is:

> Local pleasure or coherence change is not identical to long-term value or directionality. It may support directionality only when it is anchored to repairability, corrective update, continuity, and future option preservation. Pleasure and suffering should therefore be tested asymmetrically rather than treated as mirror images on one valence scale.

In this form, the theory distinguishes:

- `C`: local coherence.
- `dC/dt`: local coherence flux, a qualified local pleasure signal.
- `Omega`: directionality potential, the capacity to revise, repair, reverse, and reorganize future trajectories.
- `dOmega/dt`: long-term directionality flux.

The theory does not currently identify `dC/dt` with `dOmega/dt`.

## What The Theory Is Not Claiming

The current evidence does not support the following claims:

- VOT is proven.
- The Pleasure Order is empirically validated.
- Omega has been directly measured.
- pleasure proves order or directionality.
- distress reduction proves directionality gain.
- `dC/dt = dOmega/dt`.
- O/M/A has been directly validated as a distinct measurement architecture.
- VOT has been shown to be distinct from free-energy theory, viability theory, capability theory, SDT, SOC, or appraisal theory.
- civilization-scale or scale-invariant claims have been empirically validated.

## Current Evidence

Existing evidence has three bounded roles.

### 1. D2/D2+ Proxy Evidence

Existing survey datasets were re-expressed using an O/M/A-style grammar and related to wellbeing or distress outcomes.

This supports only:

> Exploratory/proxy association between O/M/A-like indicators and wellbeing/distress outcomes.

It does not directly validate O/M/A, pleasure, suffering, or Omega.

### 2. MIDUS/GSS Existing-Data Audits

Existing-data audits found a small and stable MIDUS distress-side signal for deficit/unanchored-deficit terms. GSS did not strongly reproduce this pattern.

This supports only:

> A narrow preliminary motivation for the distress-side unanchored-deficit hypothesis.

It does not validate the full theory.

### 3. L4 Structural-Proxy Evidence

L4 time-series work provides rule-conditional structural-proxy evidence for distinguishing repair-like from sustained non-repair/reorganization-like patterns under fixed gates.

This supports only:

> Structural analogy or proxy evidence.

It is not direct measurement of Omega, pleasure, suffering, or O/M/A.

## Current Best Hypothesis

The strongest currently testable hypothesis is not a grand theory of pleasure.

It is the narrower hypothesis:

```text
Pleasure side:
  ordered/restorative/integrative/opening pleasure should be associated
  with anchored gain.

Suffering side:
  distress/suffering should be associated with unanchored loss or deficit
  beyond general negative affect and established domain predictors.

Bridge side:
  local coherence/pleasure flux should predict later directionality only
  when supported by repairability, corrective update, continuity,
  future option preservation, and absence of harmful rigid order.
```

## Next Decisive Tests

### Test 1: Direct D3 Measurement

Directly measure:

- Order
- Meaning
- Agency
- Anchor
- ordered pleasure
- unanchored suffering

Required gates:

1. O/M/A must separate psychometrically from one general factor.
2. O/M/A + Anchor must add predictive value beyond SDT, SOC, PANAS, perceived control, and meaning.
3. An asymmetric pleasure/suffering model must outperform a simple general wellbeing/affect model.
4. Results must reproduce in a holdout or preregistered test.

### Test 2: DC2 / EMA Bridge

Lagged data are required to test the bridge specified in [Bridge Specification](BRIDGE_SPEC.md):

```text
dC_dt_lag -> later_directionality
```

This test must use frozen windows:

- `T1`: local coherence/pleasure flux.
- `T2`: persistence, corrective update, continuity, future option preservation, harmful-order gate.
- `T3`: independent directionality endpoint.

Without this test, the theory remains a two-layer framework rather than a unified valence-directionality theory.

### Test 3: Head-To-Head Distinctiveness

Even a positive D3 result would not by itself prove distinctiveness.

The comparison plan is stated in [Distinctiveness Head-to-Head](DISTINCTIVENESS_HEAD_TO_HEAD.md). The theory must be compared against:

- free-energy / predictive processing accounts of valence
- viability theory
- capability approach
- self-determination theory
- sense of coherence
- appraisal theory
- resilience / antifragility frameworks

If those frameworks explain the same results as well as VOT, then VOT should be treated as a synthesis, redescription, or parameterization, not a distinct theory.

## What Would Strengthen VOT

VOT would become stronger if:

- purpose-built O/M/A items separate cleanly;
- O/M/A + Anchor add holdout prediction beyond strong competitors;
- Unanchored_Deficit predicts suffering/distress more than ordered pleasure;
- Anchored_Gain predicts ordered/restorative/integrative/opening pleasure more than suffering;
- external datasets reproduce the distress-side unanchored-deficit increment;
- the Candidate 2 bridge beats `dC/dt` alone, affect alone, generic trend, and rival bridge models;
- VOT terms add beyond free-energy, viability, capability, SDT, SOC, and appraisal variables;
- harmful high-coherence states are correctly blocked from positive directionality scoring.

## What Would Weaken Or Downgrade VOT

VOT should be downgraded if:

- direct O/M/A fails repeatedly with purpose-built items;
- O/M/A + Anchor add no value beyond SDT/SOC/PANAS/control/meaning;
- Unanchored_Deficit fails across suitable external datasets;
- `dC/dt` alone or rival models explain later directionality as well as the bridge;
- free-energy, viability, capability, SDT, SOC, or appraisal models absorb VOT;
- harmful high-coherence states cannot be separated from positive directionality cases.

Possible downgrade labels:

- measurement vocabulary
- synthesis of existing theories
- two-layer framework without validated bridge
- unsupported conceptual framework

## Why This Is Not "Pleasure Proves Order"

The current theory explicitly rejects the simple claim that pleasure proves order.

Local pleasure can be misleading. It may reflect:

- short-term reward capture
- addictive loops
- rigid certainty
- coercive order
- brittle optimization
- reduced prediction error through closure rather than genuine future possibility

Therefore pleasure is treated as a fallible local signal. It is directionality-relevant only when anchored to repair, update, continuity, and future options.

## Why Free-Energy And Other Rival Theories Matter

Some earlier VOT formulations are close to free-energy or predictive-processing valence accounts, especially where valence is written as something like reduction in uncertainty, entropy, surprisal, or prediction error.

That overlap is not a minor issue. It is a central distinctiveness test.

The current safe position is:

> VOT is compatible with these frameworks, but it has not yet shown that it adds unique empirical value beyond them.

The theory becomes distinctive only if its anchor, asymmetry, harmful-order, and directionality-bridge terms do work that nearby theories do not already do.
