# Restart Status

This document summarizes the current restart status of VOT / The Pleasure Order.

## Current Center

The current center is definition, claim-boundary, and empirical-readiness structure.

VOT / The Pleasure Order is being organized as a conceptual reconstruction layer. It is not currently presented as empirical validation or proof.

Current restart work is limited to:

- clarifying public definitions;
- stating claim boundaries;
- identifying empirical-readiness requirements;
- separating active restart material from historical Omega material.

## Status Of Omega Materials

Earlier Omega empirical and reproducibility materials are historical, methodological, or exploratory unless explicitly reauthorized.

They should not be read as current validation of Omega. Old reproduction repositories, notebooks, CSV templates, and executable examples are archive or execution records, not the current project center.

Omega is not removed from the project, but its current status is historical, methodological, or future-conditional rather than a current empirical result.

## Validation Status

Omega validation remains blocked.

Any future empirical claim would require, before evaluation:

- fixed definitions;
- independent event labels;
- non-circularity checks;
- sufficient sample size;
- a validation design.

This restart status does not add an empirical claim.

## Public Routing

Public materials should point to this restart surface before older Omega-facing execution, reproducibility, or result materials are interpreted. Older Omega-facing materials should remain labeled as historical, methodological, or exploratory unless explicitly reauthorized.
